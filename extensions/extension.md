## GNOME Shell Extensions I Use

A  list of GNOME Shell extensions that improve productivity, usability, and visual polish.

---

### 1. Dash to Dock  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/307/dash-to-dock/)  
Transforms the GNOME dash into a fully customizable dock. Adds features like auto-hide, click actions, and position control.

---

### 2. Just Perfection  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3843/just-perfection/)  
Hide or tweak animations, panel items, workspace behavior, and more.

---

### 3. Places Status Indicator  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/8/places-status-indicator/)  
Adds quick access to common folders (Home, Documents, Downloads, etc.) directly from the top panel.

---

### 4. Clipboard Indicator  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/779/clipboard-indicator/)  
Keeps a history of copied text and lets you quickly reuse previous clipboard entries.

---

### 5. System Monitor  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/6807/system-monitor/)  
Displays real-time system stats like CPU, RAM and network usage in the top bar.

---

### 6. AppIndicator and KStatusNotifierItem Support  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/615/appindicator-support/)  
Restores tray icons for legacy and Electron apps that rely on system indicators.

---

### 7. Blur My Shell  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3193/blur-my-shell/)  
Adds modern blur effects to the overview, panel, and application backgrounds for a cleaner look.

---

### 8. Caffeine  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/517/caffeine/)  
Prevents the system from sleeping or dimming the screen when you need it to stay awake.

---

### 9. User Themes  
[Go to extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/19/user-themes/)  
This is must for using cutom themes. It allows loading custom GNOME Shell themes.

---

I use these extensions for my gnome desktop (`Fedora Workstation`) better look and feel.
